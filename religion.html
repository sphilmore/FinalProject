Week's Topic: The Existence of God

The Five Proofs Of The Existence of God by Ed Feser

The Five Proof of The Existence of God by Edward Feser presents five powerful arguments for God from classical theist perspective. The five arguments are an updated of the philosophical proofs for God presented by Aristotle, Thomas Aquinas, Plotinus, Augustine, and Leibniz.
